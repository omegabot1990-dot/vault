#### Blues

- Embellishing Minor Pentatonic
	- Minor Pentatonic : 1 - ♭3 - 4 - 5 - ♭7
	- Embellished by,
		- One of the blues notes, ==♭5==
		- Passing note, as a bridge from ♭7 to 1, ==7==
	- Major 7th blues scale also known as *Bebop blues scale*
	- Works over minor and major key blues progressions
