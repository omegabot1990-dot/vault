## Sounds and Presets

1. Instruments
	1. Instrument Rack
	2. Analog
	3. Operator
	4. Simpler
		1. Can take any one-shot sample and make it an instrument of sorts
		2. For example, Sine wave
		3. Slice
		4. Pitch Envelope
		5. Take care of Click Sounds
			1. Fade In
			2. Fade Out
		6. Gate 
			1. Cue
		7. Trigger
			1. Cue + Play
		8. Reverse
	5. Sampler
		1. Sustain 
		2. Crossfade
		3. Reverse
2. Drums
	1. Drum Rack
3. Audio Effects ==(DROP RIGHT)==
	1. Utility (always at the very end)
		1. Useful for *Gain* automation
		2. Saved in Default MIDI and Audio Tracks (right click on track title bar)
	2. EQ 3 (best at the end, before compressor)
	3. EQ 8 (best at the end, before compressor)
	4. Reverb
	5. Delay (before reverb)
	6. Compressor (comes at the end after EQ)
		1. Sidechain to duck other signals
		2. Brings the sound (Audio from) to the forefront compared to the compressed (ducked) Device
	7. Auto Filter
4. MIDI Effects ==(DROP LEFT)==
	1. Pitch
	2. Velocity
	3. Chord
	4. Arpeggiator
5. ==Plugins?==
6. Macro Knobs in Devices
	1. Controls things under the hood
	2. Can be expanded
7. Controls
	1. Dry/Wet
	2. Feedback
	3. Gain
	4. Attack
	5. Decay
	6. Sustain
	7. Release
	8. Filter
	9. Transpose
	10. Warp
		1. It is important while working with one-shot samples
		2. Tones
		3. Beats
		4. Warp Markers
	11. LFO
	12. Velocity
	13. Rate
		1. Quarter or Eighth, etc
	14. Gate
		1. Has to do with the length of the note played
	15. Legato
	16. Pitch Envelope
