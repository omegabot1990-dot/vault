## Extended Chords

- Major 9th
	- 1 - 3 - 5 - 7 - 9
	- Example: maj9, M9
- Dominant 9th
	- 1 - 3 - 5 - ♭7 - 9
	- Example: 9, dom9
- Minor 9th
	- 1 - ♭3 - 5 - ♭7 - 9
	- Example: min9, m9
- 11th
	- 1 - 3 - 5 - ♭7 - 9 - 11
	- Example: 11
- Major 13th
	- 1 - 3 - 5 - 7 - 9 - (11) - 13
	- Example: maj13, M13
	- Leave out 11th
- Dominant 13th
	- 1 - 3 - 5 - ♭7 - 9 - (11) - 13
	- Example: 13, dom13
	- Leave out 11th
- Minor 13th
	- 1 - ♭3 - 5 - ♭7 - 9 - (11) - 13
	- Example: min13, m13
	- Leave out 11th
- Added Tone Chords
	- Add 9
		- 1 - 3 - 5 - 9
		- Example: add9
		- No 7th
	- Add 13
		- 1 - 3 - 5 - 13
		- Example: add13/add6
		- No 7th
	- Add 9/13, 9/6
		- 1 - 3 - 5 - 9 - 13
		- Example: add6/9, add9/6, add13/9, add9/13
		- No 7th
==9th is same as 2nd
11th is same as 4th
13th is same as 6th
