## Chord Progression

- Combining (orÂ _harmonising_) the melody with some chords helps to fill out the texture.
- A series of chords played in a particular order. 
- In many types of music, chord progressions create a sense of tension and release by moving away from the tonic of the song and then back again.

> Example: [[202501292224 - mary had a little lamp|mary had a little lamb]]

## Basic Progressions

- Basic Pop
	- I V vi IV
		- This progression of four chords forms the basis of hundreds of ==pop songs== from the last few decades.
		- [History of Pop](https://youtu.be/HTYrkOZ5nCs)
- Basic Blues
	- Minor
		- i i i i
		- iv iv i i
		- v iv i v
	- Major
		- I I I I
		- IV IV I I
		- V IV I V
- Daft Punk
	- i III v iv
