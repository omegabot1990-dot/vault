## Exercises
==Goal: 95bpm | Now: 75bpm==
1. Picking
	1. Quarter notes
		1. *1 pick/note*
	2. Eighth notes
		1. *2 picks/note*
		2. *1 pick/note*
	3. Sixteenth notes
		1. *4 picks/note*
		2. *1 pick/note*
2. Timing/Accuracy ==(focus on this for the next two days [29th Jan])==
	1. Spider 
		1. 2 Strings
		2. Cross String
	2. Side Walk
		1. 1 String
			1. All Shapes
		2. 2 String
3. Fretboard Vision - Pentatonic (Major / Minor) (Shapes - C | A | G | E | D / Boxes)
	1. Linear
	2. Stagger
		1. 1
		2. 2
		3. 3
	3. Skip 
		1. 1
	4. Arpeggio
	5. Pedal
		1. Root
		2. 3rd
		3. 5th
	6. Extended Stretches
4. Rhythm - Chords
	1. Down (1 2 3 4)
	2. Down + Up (1 & 2 & 3 & 4 &)
	3. Down + Up (1 & _ & 3 & _ &)
	4. Down + Up (1 & 2 & _ & 4 & _ & 2 & _ & 4 &)
	5. Down + Up (1 _ 2 & _ & 4 &)
5. Structure - Progressions (Major / Minor)
	1. Basic Pop
		1. Minor
			1. i v VI vi
		2. Major
			1. I V vi IV
	2. Basic Blues
		1. Minor
			1. i i i i
			2. iv iv i i
			3. v iv i v
		2. Major
			1. I I I I
			2. IV IV I I
			3. V IV I V
