### üß† 1. **General Information**

- **App name / Developer / Parent company**
    
- **Country/region of origin**
    
- **Launch year / Active user base (if available)**
    
- **App category / target audience** (e.g. therapy companion, journaling, CBT-based, meditation, AI chat)
    

---

### üåç 2. **Region, Language, and Localisation**

- **Supported languages** (specifically, which Indian languages)
    
- **Local content adaptation** (e.g. culturally contextual prompts, Indian festivals or stressors)
    
- **Regional availability** (Play Store/App Store access by country)
    
- **Translation quality** (manual vs machine, and whether voice interfaces support local languages)
    
- **Time zone / regional features** (like reminders synced with local hours or cultural nuances in content)
    

---

### ‚öôÔ∏è 3. **Technical Capabilities & Architecture**

- **AI integration** (LLMs, sentiment analysis, mood tracking, chatbot frameworks, etc.)
    
- **Offline capabilities** (important in India for patchy connectivity)
    
- **Integration with wearables / APIs** (Fitbit, Apple Health, etc.)
    
- **Cloud infrastructure** (e.g., AWS, GCP, on-prem, Indian servers)
    
- **Update frequency & version maturity** (signals product stability)
    

---

### üí¨ 4. **Core Features**

- **Type of support** (CBT, DBT, journaling, meditation, coaching, peer chat, professional therapy)
    
- **Personalization mechanisms** (adaptive suggestions, daily reflections, progress tracking)
    
- **Gamification** (streaks, badges, milestones)
    
- **Crisis support or escalation pathway** (e.g., helplines integrated)
    
- **Multimodal input** (text, voice, image, emotion recognition)
    
- **Community or therapist connection** (if any)
    

---

### üîí 5. **Data Handling & Privacy**

- **Data storage location** (GDPR compliance, servers in EU vs outside)
    
- **Consent process & transparency** (how clearly users are informed)
    
- **Data sharing / third-party access**
    
- **Anonymization / pseudonymization**
    
- **User control** (data deletion, export)
    
- **Security practices** (end-to-end encryption, device-level encryption)
    

---

### üßæ 6. **Business & Monetization**

- **Pricing model** (free, freemium, subscription, one-time)
    
- **Revenue streams** (ads, partnerships, data monetization)
    
- **In-app purchases or upselling practices**
    
- **Partnerships with organizations, therapists, or insurance providers**
    

---

### üß© 7. **Scientific & Clinical Validity**

- **Evidence base** (references to CBT, ACT, or research papers)
    
- **Accreditation or regulatory compliance** (e.g., HIPAA, NHS-approved)
    
- **Expert involvement** (clinical psychologists, research advisors)
    
- **Outcome tracking / validation studies**
    

---

### ‚≠ê 8. **User Experience & Engagement**

- **App store ratings and reviews** (focus on usability, trust, and localisation feedback)
    
- **Retention mechanisms** (notifications, personalised nudges)
    
- **Accessibility** (text-to-speech, colorblind-friendly UI)
    
- **User demographics (if available)** (age, region, gender focus)
    

---

### üìà 9. **Market Positioning**

- **Unique Value Proposition (UVP)**
    
- **Target segment** (students, working professionals, anxiety/depression specific)
    
- **Competitive advantage**
    
- **Brand tone / messaging**
    
- **Marketing channels & partnerships**
    

---

### Optional (if relevant to your research goals):

- **Ethical AI practices** (bias mitigation, explainability)
    
- **Localisation partnerships in India** (NGOs, universities, telehealth platforms)
    
- **Community impact metrics** (downloads in India, testimonials, media coverage)
