## Core Concepts

1. **Collection**: The process of gathering information from various sources, including:
	+ Text-based materials (articles, books, etc.)
	+ Audio-based materials (music, podcasts, etc.)
	+ Video-based materials (YouTube, etc.)
	+ Conversational exchanges

2. **Organisation**: The art of structuring and categorising your collected information to facilitate easy retrieval, including:
	+ Categorisation: grouping related ideas together
	+ Tagging: assigning keywords or labels to your notes
	+ Linking: connecting related ideas and concepts
	+ Indexing: creating a system for quickly finding specific information

3. **Processing**: The process of making sense of the information you've collected, including:
	+ Summarising: distilling key takeaways from complex information
	+ Identifying patterns: recognising connections between seemingly unrelated ideas
	+ Synthesising new ideas: combining existing knowledge to create innovative insights

4. **Sharing**: The act of sharing your knowledge and understanding with others, including: ^1411f3
	+ Writing: creating blog posts, articles, or other written content
	+ Creating content: producing videos, podcasts, or other multimedia formats
	+ Discussing ideas: engaging in online forums, social media discussions, or in-person conversations

5. **Reflection**: The process of regularly reviewing and journaling your progress to:
	+ Reinforce memory: solidifying new knowledge and skills through repetition
	+ Spark new ideas: generating innovative insights through reflection and analysis
