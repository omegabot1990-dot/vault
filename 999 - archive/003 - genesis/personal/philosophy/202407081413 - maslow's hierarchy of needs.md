## Key Points

- The order of the levels are not completely fixed.
- Our behaviours are usually motivated by multiple needs simultaneously.
- The levels can be further extended.

### Deficiency Needs

1. **Physiological Needs** - Biological requirements for human survival.
2. **Safety Needs** - Sense of order, predictability and control over life.
3. **Love and Belonging Needs** - Emotional need for interpersonal relationships, affiliating, connectedness, being part of a group/s.
4. **Esteem Needs** - Self-esteem (esteem for oneself) and the desire for respect from others.
### Growth Needs

1. **Cognitive Needs** - Knowledge and understanding. Stems curiosity, explorations and a need for meaning and predictability.
2. **Aesthetic Needs** - Appreciation and search for beauty, balance, form etc.
3. **Self-actualisation Needs** - Realising personal potential, self-fulfilment, seeking personal growth, and peak experiences.
4. **Transcendence Needs** - The human desire to connect with a higher reality, purpose, or the universe.
