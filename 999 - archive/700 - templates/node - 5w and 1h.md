1. > [!info]- **Who?**
> - [ ] Who are the main individuals or groups involved?
> - [ ] Who has an interest or stake in the outcome?
> - [ ] Who stands to gain or lose from this situation?
> - [ ] Who is responsible for specific actions or decisions?
   
2. > [!info]- **What?**
> - [ ] What is happening or has happened?
> - [ ] What are the goals or purposes?
> - [ ] What problems or challenges need to be addressed?
> - [ ] What are the expected results or impacts?

3. > [!info]- **When?**
> - [ ] When did it happen, or when will it happen?
> - [ ] When are critical milestones or deadlines?
> - [ ] When do specific actions need to occur in the sequence of events?
> - [ ] How long will the event or action take?

4. > [!info]- **Where?**
> - [ ] Where is the event taking place?
> - [ ] Where are the areas affected by this event or action?
> - [ ] Where within a particular context does this fit?
> - [ ] Where will resources and personnel be deployed?

6. > [!info]- **Why?**
> - [ ] Why is this happening? What is the objective?
> - [ ] Why is this important to the people involved or affected?
> - [ ] What are the underlying causes or reasons for this event?
> - [ ] Why should we care about the outcomes or impacts?

7. > [!info]- **How?**
> - [ ] How will the system be implemented?
> - [ ] How will success be measured?
> - [ ] How will the data be collected and analysed?
> - [ ] How will the results be used?
