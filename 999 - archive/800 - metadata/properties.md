#### Task Type

- **Capture** – Collect raw ideas, inputs, or information as they come.
- **Organise** – Sort, structure, and prioritise captured items.
- **Distil** – Extract the core meaning, insights, or next steps.
- **Plan** – Map out actions, timelines, and resources.
- **Execute** – Carry out the planned tasks to completion.
