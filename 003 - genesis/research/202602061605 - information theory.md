- Information theory studies <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">encoding</mark>, <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">decoding</mark>, <mark style="background: #ADCCFFA6;">transmitting</mark>, and <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">manipulating</mark> information [^1]
- Information theory provides fundamental language for discussing the information processing in various systems [^2]
- The Shannon Model
	1. Source
		1. Generates the message
	2. Encoder
		1. Converts message into signals/bits
	3. Channel
		1. The medium (subject to noise)
	4. Decoder
		1. Reconstructs the message from the signal
	5. Receiver
		1. The destination of the message
- Applications
	- Data Compression
		- ZIP, MP3, and JPEG files
	- Networking
		- 5G, Wi-Fi, and deep-space communication
	- Cryptography
		- Measuring the unpredictability of encryption keys
	- Machine Learning
