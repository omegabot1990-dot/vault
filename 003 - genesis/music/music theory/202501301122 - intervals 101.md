## Key Concepts

- ==What is an *interval*?==
  The distance between two notes/pitches.
- What is a *whole-step/whole tone or W/T*?
  The interval is two notes/pitches.
- What is *half-step/semitone or H/S*?
  The interval is one note/pitch.
- What are *accidentals*?
  Symbols that are used to raise or lower the pitch of a note.
