## Augmented Chord

- 1 - 3 - ♯5, triad
- Example: Caug, C+

## Diminished Chord

- 1 - ♭3 - ♭5, triad
- Example: Cdim, C°
- WH / WH
	- Example: B° - B D F, *BD - WH* and *DF - WH*
