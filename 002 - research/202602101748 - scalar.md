- A scalar is a single number
	- Usually a real number, e.g. $x \in \mathbb{R}$
- Scalars are the simplest objects we build vectors and matrices from
- In ML code, a scalar is often stored as a tensor with shape `()`

> [!MATH] Scalar
> $x \in \mathbb{R}$
> 
> - Usual arithmetic: $+$, $-$, $\times$, $\div$, exponentiation
