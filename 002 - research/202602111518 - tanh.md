- The hyperbolic tangent maps a real number to the interval $(-1,1)$
- It is smooth, odd, and often used as a zero-centered squashing function

> [!MATH] Hyperbolic tangent $\tanh(x)$
> The tanh function is
> $$\tanh(x)=\frac{e^x-e^{-x}}{e^x+e^{-x}}$$

- Relation to sigmoid
  - $$\tanh(x)=2\sigma(2x)-1$$

- Limits
  - $\tanh(x)\to1$ as $x\to\infty$
  - $\tanh(x)\to-1$ as $x\to-\infty$

> [!MATH] Derivative of tanh
> The derivative is
> $$\frac{d}{dx}\tanh(x)=1-\tanh^2(x)$$

- The slope is largest at $x=0$, where $\tanh(0)=0$
