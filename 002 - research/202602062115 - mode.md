- The Mode is simply the <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">value that appears most often</mark> in a dataset
	- The most frequent observation in the set
- Uniqueness
	- A dataset can have one mode, multiple modes (bimodal/multimodal), or no mode at all
- Categorical Data
	- This is the <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">only measure of central tendency that works for non-numerical data</mark> (e.g., "What is the most popular car colour?")
