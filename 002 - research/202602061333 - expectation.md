- For a discrete [[202602061340 - random variable|random variable]] $X$, the expectation is the <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">sum of every possible value multiplied by its probability</mark> [^1]

> [!MATH] Expectation
> $$E[X] = \sum_{i=1}^{n} x_i P(X=x_i)$$
> 
> - **$x_i$**: The possible outcome (e.g., heads or tails in a coin toss)
> - **$P(x_i)$**: How likely that outcome is (e.g., 50%)
