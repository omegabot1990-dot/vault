- Learning rate is a [[202602111256 - hyperparameters|hyperparameter]] that controls the step size in [[202602111314 - model parameters|parameter]] updates
- In [[202602111605 - optimization algorithms|gradient-based optimisation]], it scales how far parameters move along the negative gradient
- Too high can cause <mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">instability</mark>, <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">oscillation</mark>, or <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">divergence</mark>
- Too low can make [[202602111335 - training|training]] very slow or stall progress
- [[202602111617 - learning rate schedule|Learning rate schedules]] change this value over training to improve convergence
