- A vector is an ordered list of numbers
- You can think of it as a point in space or a direction you can move
- Vectors are used to store data and represent features in ML

> [!MATH] Vector
> $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^n$
> 
> $$\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix}x_1 \\ \vdots \\ x_n\end{bmatrix}$$
